About Me

Who am I?

Hello! My name is Gagandeep Kang and I'm currently a junior studying Computer Science with a minor in Mathematics from Boston University.

During the school year, I have the opportunity to be a part of Boston University's Hack4Impact Chapter, which is an organization that develops applications for non-profits in Boston. Within Hack4Impact, I co-founded the Junior Development team which provides an opportunity for students who are interested in full-stack development to learn through a project-based model. I am also the President of BU's Global App Initiative which builds mobile apps for non-profits in the local Boston community.
