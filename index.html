About Me

Who am I?

Hello! My name is Gagandeep Kang and I'm currently a sophomore studying Computer Science and Mathematics at Boston University.

During the school year, I have the opportunity to be a part of Boston University's Hack4Impact Chapter, which is an organization that develops applications for non-profits in Boston. I am also the treasurer of BU's Global App Initiative which is an organization that builds apps for our community. This past semester, I joined OpenWeb, an app incubator that brings together highly motivated students to develop tech projects to collaborate, design, and learn together.

Outside of developing, I am a judge for the Boston Debate League, am a food fanatic, and love playing and watching basketball.
