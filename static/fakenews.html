Fake News Chrome Extension

About the Project

Fake News is a Google Chrome Browser Extension that helps check the authenticity of news articles online. Users can click the extension button to analyze the headline of the article they are viewing.

The extension uses a Natural Language Processing Model built with Google Cloud to compare the headline to known Fake News headlines. It also uses a PageRank API to check the relevance of the website and offers articles from other sources relating to the keywords of the headline.
