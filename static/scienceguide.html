ScienceGuide Mobile Application

About the Project

ScienceGuide is a mobile application that attempts to connect Latin American High School students who are looking to start research and interested in competing in Science Fairs with experience mentors. The project was developed during the BU Spark Fellowship program in Spring 2020. Application features include custom mentor curation based on research interests and skill along with in-app chat functionality to connect with mentors. Application is available for iOS and Android
