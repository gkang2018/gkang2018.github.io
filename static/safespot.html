SafeSpot Web Application

About the Project

SafeSpot is a web application meant to provide users the optimal time to visit a business based on the venue's total capacity and current occupancy. Especially during the pandemic, it is crucial to ensure that one is visitng public places in a safe way and our application recognizes that. Built with VueJS for our front-end and a Flask / SQL back-end, SafeSpot is the perfect application to help plan your business visits.
